Welcome to Antibody!

In this game, your objective is to protect the Nexus from the arriving enemies.
To do this, you can place different structures on the map (left click), to reroute their path, and defeat them.
You can remove structures by right clicking.
The structures you can build are:
- Wall: Does nothing but block the enemies' path.
- Gun turret: Attacks by shooting bullets at an enemy. High range, Average damage.
- Laser turret: Attacks by continuously shooting a laser at an enemy. Low range, high damage.
- AoE Tower: Attacks all enemies in range, but does so slowly. Average range, Average Damage per enemy.
